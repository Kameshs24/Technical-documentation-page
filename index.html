Jewellery saving plans

Jewellery savings plans are savings schemes offered by various jewellery partners that allow you to save a particular amount over a tenure, and after the end of the tenure, you can purchase jewellery or gold coins from the jewellery partners, often with added benefits or discounts.

There are different categories of savings plans in the zeed app, such as Gold, Silver, Diamond, Platinum, Gold Coins and Bars. Also, there are one time deposit savings plans, Old Jewel deposits savings plan and occasion based special savings plan. These plans may vary from partner to partner.

Offer & Discounts

Wastage :

  • Wastage charges are levied to compensate for gold lost during the jewellery-making process.Wastage charges assist jewellers in recovering the cost of lost gold. The percentage of waste might vary based on the jewellery partner policies and the complexity of the design.

Making charge :

  • Making charge is the cost of the process involved in producing and designing any piece of gold jewellery that you buy. Simply put, it is the manufacturing cost to change raw gold into fine jewellery.

Value added (V.A) :

  • The Wastage Charges and Making charges together are called VA or 'Value Addition'.
Redemption

As the tenure of your savings plan is complete, you should visit the partner store and branch you selected when starting the savings plan. Approach the Jewellery saving plan redemption section and provide them with your mobile number and your plan ID. The Jewellery partner will then inform you about the details of the redemptions. You can confirm the redemption by sharing your OTP with the Jewellery partner. Once the OTP is verified, an invoice will be generated, and you can purchase jewellery for the invoiced value of Gold or Amount.

Cancellation & Refunds

You can cancel your savings plan according to the cancellation and refund policies of our Jewellery partners.

Your refund will either be in the form of a cash refund, or you can use the saved value to purchase jewellery from the jewellery partner. The method of refund is solely dependent on the refund and cancellation policy of our jewellery partner, which is mentioned on each partner's page.
